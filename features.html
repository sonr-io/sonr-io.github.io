  • Shift the Paradigm for file sharing.

Universal

Send files from any device to any device by using a combination of Ultrasonic Sound, Bluetooth, and Rendezvous Discovery in order to find all the available devices in the room.

Point and Share

Because of Sonr's advanced algorithm for discovering devices, users can just point at any one of their devices to start a transfer.

Secure

Sonr is built completely decentralized. All Peer Routing and File Transfer's are completely P2P with no external servers involved. When you share files with Sonr an encrypted unique channel is made with only the two devices.

Contact Cards

Let's be honest, native mobile contact cards are awful. With Sonr's In App Editor, you can share Contact Cards or Create your own Portfolio.

Home Screen

Shows all your recently received cards and files.

Peer Details

Shows all your recently received cards and files.

Media Picker

Load up a file directly from Sonr or any other App.

Compass View

All the devices around you appear based on their position around you.
